About Eidhl

Born in August 2021 as "HR Tech City", our platform was reimagined and rebranded to Eidhl in April 2025 — a name that reflects a broader global vision. What began as a tech niche evolved into a knowledge-driven sanctuary.

Today, Eidhl is a nonprofit digital platform that publishes articles, news, and deep educational content across technology, world affairs, business, education, travel, sports, history, science, and global trends. Our mission: democratize knowledge and spark curiosity worldwide.

Headquarters: Dhaka · Founder: Habibur Rahman
